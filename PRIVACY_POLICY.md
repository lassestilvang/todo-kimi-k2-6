# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 2026

## Introduction

TaskFlow respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, and avatar when you create an account
- **Tasks and Content**: Tasks, notes, labels, and other content you create
- **Calendar Data**: If you connect your calendar, we access event data to sync tasks
- **Email Addresses**: For task sharing and notifications

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data**: How you use the app (features, frequency, duration)
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
- **Log Data**: IP address, timestamps, API requests

## How We Use Your Information

### Core Functionality

- **Task Management**: Create, read, update, and delete your tasks
- **Calendar Sync**: Sync tasks with your Google or Outlook calendar
- **Sharing**: Share tasks and views with other users
- **Notifications**: Send email reminders for due tasks

### Improvement and Analytics

- **Product Development**: Understand how users interact with features
- **Performance Monitoring**: Detect and fix performance issues
- **Error Tracking**: Identify and resolve bugs

## Data Storage and Security

### Data Location

- Your data is stored in a SQLite database (development) or PostgreSQL (production)
- Data is stored in the EU or US depending on your location

### Security Measures

- **Encryption**: Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
- **Authentication**: Secure authentication with NextAuth.js
- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions for shared tasks
- **Rate Limiting**: Protection against abuse

## Your Rights

### Access and Portability

- **View Your Data**: You can see all your data through the app
- **Export Data**: Export all your data in JSON format at any time
- **Delete Data**: Delete your account and all associated data

### Correction and Deletion

- **Update Information**: Correct your profile information anytime
- **Delete Tasks**: Delete individual tasks or all tasks
- **Account Deletion**: Request complete account deletion

### Analytics

We may use analytics services to understand usage patterns. This data is anonymous and aggregated.

## Children's Privacy

TaskFlow is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
